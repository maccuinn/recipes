Ingredients:
  1 cup of long grain rice
  1 cup of coconut milk
  1 cup of water
  1/2 tsp salt
  3/4 tsp of sugar

Steps:
  combine ingredients in a saucepan over high heat. cover with a lid and bring
  to a boil.
  turn down heat to low then leave to simmer for 15-18 min until all the liquid
  has absorbed.
  remove from heat and allow to rest for at least 5 minutes before serving.
